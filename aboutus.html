Our Story: From a Small Project to Building the Future

Every great company starts with a spark. For us, that spark was a challenging client project that proved the power of data, automation, and AI.

The Genesis Project: One Delta LLC

Our journey began in the summer of 2025 with a pivotal contract for an ambitious U.S.-based company.

A Global Vision, A Local Problem

Our client, **One Delta LLC** in Atlanta, Georgia, had a compelling vision: to export fresh Indian vegetables to the U.S. to serve the Indian diaspora. Sourcing from India offered a significant advantage with lower tariffs compared to Mexico. To make this venture viable, they needed a sophisticated software solution.

  • **Advanced Data Analytics:** The company needed a system to compute complex pricing metrics—including 52-day, 30-day, and 90-day moving averages, and median prices—spanning from January 2022 to the present.
  • **Real-time Market Data:** The core of the solution was the ability to pull real-time data from the bustling Chennai vegetable market.
  • **Predictive Intelligence:** We were tasked with plotting this data on a graph, making meaningful market projections, and tracking weather conditions to aid in critical planning.

Building India's AI Infrastructure

The success of our first project sparked a new realization: a significant number of businesses in India—especially MSMEs and startups—were not leveraging modern technology. We saw an opportunity to build the AI infrastructure they were missing.

Our First Steps in Hospitality

We chose to begin with the hospitality sector, a perfect fit for automation and efficiency gains. We dove deep into the industry, collaborating with **Property Management System (PMS)** providers like **Ezee** to understand the landscape. Our detailed market research identified critical technology gaps that were holding businesses back.

Despite facing significant challenges like **limited funding, poor partner experiences, and a small founding team**, our determination never wavered. We knew the potential was immense. We turned our focus to hands-on projects with two local partners, which gave us the invaluable insights needed to build our core product.

Our Vision: The Intelligent Operating System for Modern Hospitality

We envision a future where every hotel operates with peak efficiency, staff are empowered by technology, and every guest experience is seamlessly exceptional. This isn't about adding another tool; it's about building the future of hospitality.

The Constant Juggle of Hotel Management

Running a hotel today is a relentless balancing act between rising costs and guest expectations. The old way of managing operations is no longer sustainable.

Rising Costs

Owners and managers are caught between rising operational costs and the pressure to deliver more.

Overworked Staff

Staff are stretched thin, handling repetitive tasks instead of focusing on high-value guest service.

Missed Opportunities

The constant pressure leads to burnout, costly errors, and missed opportunities for revenue.

An Autonomous, Empowered Future

Our vision is to build the intelligent operating system for Indian hospitality—a future where management is autonomous, staff are empowered, and profitability is effortless.

We see a world where the hotel's day-to-day operations run on an invisible, intelligent engine. An AI that anticipates guest needs, orchestrates staff duties, and optimizes revenue in the background.

By automating the mundane, we free hotel staff from transactional tasks and empower them to become true hosts. They are no longer bottlenecks; they are supervisors of an efficient system, with more time to focus on what humans do best: providing genuine, memorable hospitality that turns guests into lifelong advocates.
