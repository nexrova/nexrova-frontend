Our Story

How our first real-world project laid the foundation for Agentic AI.

Our Beginning

Our founding team’s journey began with a project for One Delta LLC, Atlanta, Georgia (a paid client) over the summer of 2025.

The Challenge

One Delta LLC planned to launch a business exporting Indian vegetables via cold storage to the U.S. market—bringing the authentic taste of home to Indians living abroad. Exporting from Mexico had higher tariffs, so they decided to source from India.

As a starting point for this project, the company’s President wanted a software solution that could:

  • Compute 52-vegetable DMA, 30-day DMA, 90-day DMA, and median prices (from Jan 1, 2022 to present)
  • Pull real-time data from the Chennai vegetable market (Web Scraped)
  • Plot this data on a graph
  • Make meaningful projections for market research using data analytical algorithms.
  • Track weather conditions to aid in planning

Our Solution

We developed this solution, and that marked our first major contract.

Deployment & Current Status

This project was given into 4 Phases and the project is now active and running from a VPS (deployed by the founding team) at bazy.in.

Project Snapshot

  • Client: One Delta LLC
  • Location: Atlanta, Georgia
  • Timeline: Summer 2025
  • Focus: Data, Analytics, Automation
  • Deployment: VPS at bazy.in

Impact

A production-grade analytics and forecasting tool powering real-time sourcing and market research decisions.
