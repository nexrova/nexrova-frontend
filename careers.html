Careers at Nexrova

Shape the Future of AI in Hospitality

At Nexrova, we’re not just building software—we’re redefining how hotels interact with their guests. Our AI-first approach automates guest services from check-in and room management to personalized support, freeing staff to focus on what truly matters: exceptional hospitality.

By joining us, you’ll be part of a team innovating at the intersection of technology and human experience. Your work will directly influence hotel operations, improve efficiency, and elevate guest satisfaction across India—and beyond.

Whether you’re developing advanced AI algorithms, crafting seamless user experiences, or optimizing backend systems, every contribution helps shape a smarter, more connected hospitality ecosystem.

Be part of the change. Build the future. Transform hospitality.

Why Intern at Nexrova?

Make Real-World Impact

Your code, research, and ideas won’t sit on the shelf. You’ll directly contribute to our core product, helping independent hotels thrive.

Work on the Cutting Edge

Gain hands-on experience with Large Language Models (LLMs), scalable backend systems, and modern full-stack development that solves real business problems.

Be More Than Just an Intern

At Nexrova, your voice matters. Work closely with founders, take ownership of projects, and gain startup-level exposure.

Grow with a Pioneering Team

Mentorship and growth are built into our culture. Learn the ins and outs of building a B2B SaaS company—technically and strategically.

Open Internship Roles

Backend Developer Intern – AI/LLM

Skills: Python, LangChain/LLM APIs (OpenAI/Anthropic), FastAPI/Flask, Redis (preferred), Git/GitHub

Role: Build AI-powered conversational flows for WhatsApp-based hotel automation. Connect LLMs with real hotel workflows for smooth, scalable interactions.

Apply Now

Backend Developer Intern – Systems & Integrations

Skills: Python/Node.js, REST APIs & webhooks, SQL databases, Authentication/Security (JWT/OAuth), Docker (preferred)

Role: Ensure backend reliability and integrations. Connect APIs (WhatsApp Cloud, payments), design scalable databases, and secure infrastructure.

Apply Now

Full-Stack Developer Intern

Skills: React/Next.js, Node.js/Python, SQL databases, API integration, UI/UX fundamentals (Figma a plus)

Role: Build the admin dashboard, connecting backend services with a clean, user-friendly interface for hotel staff.

Apply Now

Operations & Business Research Intern

Skills: Research & analytics, documentation/presentation, market & competitor analysis, SaaS/hospitality knowledge (plus), strong communication

Role: Support business strategy. Research industry trends, benchmark competitors, and assist with documentation, case studies, and pilot program materials.

Apply Now

Not Fully Qualified? Don’t Worry.

We value potential over credentials. If you’re passionate, committed, and ready to work on real projects, we’ll help you upskill along the way. Every intern goes through a probation period with hands-on training, mentorship, and guidance to help you grow while contributing to building the next generation of AI-powered hospitality solutions.
