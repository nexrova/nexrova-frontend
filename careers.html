Careers at Nexrova

Build the Future of AI in Hospitality

At Nexrova, we’re shaping how hotels and service apartments operate in the age of artificial intelligence. Our SaaS platform automates guest check-ins, support, and payments—helping businesses reduce costs, boost revenue, and deliver exceptional hospitality experiences.

We believe internships should be meaningful. That’s why at Nexrova, you won’t just observe—you’ll contribute to live projects that directly impact our clients and their guests.

Joining us means being part of a fast-growing startup where your work has visibility, purpose, and long-term value.

Why Join as an Intern at Nexrova?

Learn Beyond the Classroom

Work with cutting-edge technologies: AI agents, LangChain, FastAPI, SaaS platforms, and WhatsApp automation. These are the skills top companies are actively hiring for.

Real Projects, Real Impact

Your contributions won’t be side projects. You’ll help build solutions powering actual hotels across India—from automated check-ins to AI-driven guest support.

Startup Culture, High Ownership

We move fast. At Nexrova, interns are treated as contributors, not assistants. Your ideas can shape features and reach production within weeks.

Flexibility for Students

Our internships are remote-friendly and outcome-based, designed to fit alongside your academic schedule.

Career Growth

Stand out in placements by showcasing impactful work. Top performers also receive recommendation letters, endorsements, and priority consideration for future paid roles.
