An AI-Powered OS for the Modern Hotel

Nexrova isn't just another tool; it's a complete automation layer that works with your existing systems. Discover how our core features transform your operations, enhance guest experiences, and drive revenue.

Core Platform Features

Our platform is built on a foundation of powerful, interconnected AI tools that automate every facet of your hotel.

AI Receptionist (24/7)

Handles bookings, FAQs, and automates check-ins via WhatsApp. Drives revenue by intelligently upselling services like late checkouts, meals, and taxis.

AI Staff Assistant

Automates task assignments for housekeeping, sends reminders for check-outs, and routes guest requests (e.g., extra towels) to the correct staff instantly.

Owner's Dashboard

Provides real-time analytics on occupancy, payments, and staff efficiency. Track every rupee of upsell revenue and make data-driven decisions from anywhere.

Seamless PMS Integration

Acts as an AI layer that connects with your existing PMS (eZee, Hotelogix, etc.) via APIs, enhancing your current system without needing replacement.
